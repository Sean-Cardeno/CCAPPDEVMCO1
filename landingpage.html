GamblerGoals
Where Productivity Meets Fun !

Enter a world where organizing tasks turns into an exciting journey to self improvement ! GamblerGoals is not just a task organizer; it's a unique experience that blends the practicality of traditional task management with the thrill of a gacha-style game.

What's Inside ?

  • Effortless Task Management: Create, track, and prioritize your tasks with ease. Set deadlines manage your progress, and stay on top of your game.
  • Engaging Gacha Simulator: Complete tasks and earn rolls to unlock collectible characters from various franchises. The more tasks you conquer, the richer your collection!
  • Weekly Challenges: Embark on weekly quests for an extra thrill. Each challenge is a chance to prove your dedication and spice up your routine.
  • Rewarding Experience System: Gain experience points for task completion. Use them to enhance your profile or increase your chances of winning favorite characters.
  • Progress Tracking: View your task history and performance. Celebrate your progress with personalized feedback and see how much MORE PRODUCTIVE you've become!
